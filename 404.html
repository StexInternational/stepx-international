Meet the Visionary Behind the Mission

Tony Saji

Managing Director, StepX International Pvt. Ltd.
Corporate Trainer | NLP Grand Master | Business Transformation Coach

Professional Overview

Hailing from Kollam, Kerala, Tony Saji is a dynamic corporate trainer and Grand Master of NLP, widely recognized for transforming business performance through structured training and strategic consulting. As Co-founder and Lead Consultant at Tonex Group, he brings over a decade of expertise in driving organizational growth through high-impact coaching in leadership, operations, and sales strategy. His sessions are known for their energy, clarity, and outcome-driven focus, making him a trusted mentor across multiple industries.

Academic & Professional Credentials

  • B.Com : Kerala University – Specialization in Accounting, Finance, and Business Law.
  • M.A. in Economics : Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) – Focus on Policy Analysis and Strategic Thinking.
  • NLP Grand Master: Certified in Neuro-Linguistic Programming at the Grand Master level.
  • STED Council: Certified by the Scientific & Technical Education Development Council – A technical and vocational education body.
  • BSS – Bharat Sevak Samaj: Certified by a national development agency under the Planning Commission for non-formal skill development.
  • TSSR Council: Certified by the Technical Skill Survey and Research Council – Focused on technical training and skill-based education.

Key Achievements

  • Honored with the India Darshan National Integration Award for Best Young Entrepreneur of the Year 2020.
  • Trained over 10,000 professionals across industries in leadership and sales.
  • Led transformation programs for 50+ organizations across India and the Middle East.
  • Recognized speaker at national-level business forums and educational summits.
  • Designed NLP-based performance modules that improved team efficiency by over 40%.
